Hi,

I'm a tenured associate professor in Institute of Modern Physics, Fudan University.
My main research field is experimental high energy nuclear physics, using heavy-ion collisions to study various phenomena, such as collective motion of QGP and exotic particles.
I'm a member of the LHC-ALICE collaboration and the deputy leader of the ALICE Shanghai group.

ALICE Shanghai group

Joining in ALICE experiment since 2019, Fudan University (Shanghai group) is one of the four institutes in China. Our current physics interests mainly lie in the study of exotic phenomena (e.g. anomalous chiral effects) and matters.
